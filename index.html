Rationale

Continuing its legacy of promoting scientific inquiry and education, the United Science Foundation (USF) proudly presents SANKALP: Scientific Activities for Novelty and Knowledge Advancements & Learning Programs. Building upon the success of the Azmalpur Science Mela organized in 2019, SANKALP represents a natural progression in our ongoing commitment to empowering rural India through science and education.
At SANKALP, we aim to further deepen our impact by offering an expanded array of activities and themes that delve into the forefront of scientific discovery and innovation. By bringing together experts, educators, and students, we seek to create a vibrant platform for learning, collaboration, and inspiration.
While honoring the spirit of the previous Science Mela, SANKALP introduces new dimensions and opportunities for engagement, including major themes such as Astronomy, Genetics, Virology, and Climate Change. These themes not only reflect the evolving landscape of scientific inquiry but also underscore our dedication to addressing contemporary challenges facing society.
In addition to building upon past successes, SANKALP also represents a fresh opportunity to reach new audiences, foster deeper connections within rural communities, and empower the next generation of scientific leaders. Through dynamic workshops, interactive discussions, and hands-on activities, we aim to spark curiosity, stimulate critical thinking, and ignite a passion for lifelong learning among participants.
As we embark on this journey of exploration and discovery, we invite you to join us in continuing the legacy of the Science Mela and ushering in a new era of scientific advancement and empowerment in rural India. Together, let us build upon the foundation laid in 2019 and chart a course towards a brighter, more inclusive future for all.
